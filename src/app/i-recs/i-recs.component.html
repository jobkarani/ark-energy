How I-RECs Work

Energy Generation

Your Solar, wind, hydro or other renewable energy powered mini-grid generates clean electricity that helps fight climate change.

Certification

Each unit (1 MWh) of this clean electricity creates an I-REC, a digital certificate proving its eco-friendly source.

Monetization

Project owners can sell these I-RECs, allowing them to continue subsiding the operating costs of their mini-grids.
