How to Get Started

01

Verify Eligibility

Ensure your renewable energy project meets the criteria for I-REC issuance, including being located in a country that participates in the I-REC Standard system (Kenya is a new member).

02

Register Your Project

Work with us to get your project registered in the I-REC system.

03

Record and Report

We will help you regularly report your energy generation data and also help you get issued with I-RECs corresponding to your clean energy production.

04

Sell Your I-RECs

Engage with our platform to store, sell and retire your certificates.
